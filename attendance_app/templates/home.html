About Our Project

The RFID and Face Recognition Based Smart Attendance System is a project developed by the students of Diploma Computer Engineering at Silver Oak University. Our primary goal is to streamline and secure attendance tracking processes using advanced technologies.

In traditional attendance systems, manual processes often lead to errors, delays, and even fraudulent entries. Our smart solution combines RFID scanning with facial recognition to automatically verify and log attendance, ensuring accuracy and security. Once a student taps their RFID tag, a live camera feed is triggered for facial authentication using OpenCV.

A successful match marks the student’s attendance in a centralized SQLite database. Our Django-based admin dashboard with modern UI elements and Excel export capabilities enables staff to generate daily, subject-wise, or complete attendance reports with ease.

This comprehensive desktop application, compiled as an executable (.bat) file, launches both the backend and face recognition modules concurrently for real-time monitoring. It reflects a significant advancement in educational technology, integrating RFID and facial recognition to offer a reliable, transparent, and scalable attendance solution.

Project Aim

To design and implement an automated attendance system that ensures accurate, real-time logging of student attendance using a combination of RFID scanning and facial recognition, while offering a user-friendly Django-based web interface for administration, tracking, and reporting.

Theme

The project’s central theme is hybrid automation and biometric security. By integrating RFID technology with facial recognition, the system reflects the evolution toward smarter, more secure digital campuses. The UI follows a modern aesthetics, equipped with real-time logs and exportable administrative controls, aligning with the vision of data-driven educational institutions.

Key Features

Automated Tracking

Instant and effortless attendance recording via RFID card or tag scans combined with facial recognition verification.

Real-time Data
